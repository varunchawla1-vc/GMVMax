How does Commission Saving via GMV Max impact my P&L?

Commission Saving via GMV Max applies a real-time platform commission reduction to eligible campaigns — lowering your commission fee on qualifying orders in exchange for a slightly lower RoI target and a higher daily ads spend, so the platform can optimise for more GMV.

This walkthrough shows exactly how that tradeoff flows through your P&L — what happens to GMV Profit Margin RoI Ads Spend, and when the maths works in your favour.

  • Actual results depend on creative supply, auction competition, inventory & stock, pricing, seasonality, audience reach and platform algorithm behaviour.

  • Commission terms, eligibility, and availability are subject to TikTok Shop's official offer and may change without notice.
  • Consult your TikTok account team and your own finance function before acting on these figures.
